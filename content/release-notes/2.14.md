This update includes big Profile enhancements, external links for media, plus other improvements and fixes:

- Profile screens have a whole lot more info, including the user's bio, favorites, followers/follows, and whether they are a moderator or admin! Plus, the profile screen now follows the user's color theme.
- External Links are available for anime and manga, where you can find project websites and social media, and official places to watch or read the media
- When looking at media details, users you follow with that media on their list have a new design that includes episode/chapter progress
- Updated the Recommendations screen to make community votes easier to understand
- Added support for submission update notifications
- Fix crashes on various screens resulting from duplicate items being present. In particular this should help on Notifications, and on staff media lists where the staff member has a lot of associated media.
- Fix vertical alignment of studio media without any genres.
- Fix invalid login tokens sometimes staying around if you cancel out of the "log back in" prompt
- Fix extra whitespace around some notification text
